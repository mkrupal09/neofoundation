Endeavouring to make our world a better place

Neo foundation is a non profit service organization established originally in 2009 in vadodara by sachin parekh AND vishaka parekh. Neo foundation had been started in India on 2/10/2009 and it subsequently evolved as a social service under the guindance of lot of expertised people.
Our mission is to support communities locally and globally, giving help and impacting lives through humanitarian service projects and grants!!
